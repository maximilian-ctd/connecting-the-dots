# GenesysAI launches on the Breuninger marketplace

## Fast marketplace launch through EAN matching and backfill

GenesysAI expanded its digital sales strategy and successfully launched on the Breuninger marketplace. Using an efficient backfill model and simple product onboarding via EAN matching, the go live was implemented without major technical effort. The entire project was supported by ConnectingTheDots, including first contact, contract closing, project management, KYC process support, and operational marketplace management.

This launch shows how brands can sell on premium marketplaces quickly, efficiently, and in a scalable way.

## Breuninger marketplace as a growth channel for brands

The Breuninger marketplace is one of the leading premium marketplaces in the DACH region and gives brands access to a high quality audience with strong purchasing power. For companies like GenesysAI, this opens up new opportunities to increase reach and unlock additional revenue potential without building their own platform infrastructure.

Benefits of the Breuninger marketplace:

* Access to an established premium community
* Higher brand visibility in the fashion and lifestyle environment
* A scalable e commerce sales channel
* Use of existing infrastructure and customer access points

## Fast product listing without technical complexity through EAN matching

A key success factor was the simple technical integration via the EAN matching and backfill model.

Products are not created from scratch but matched through existing EANs against product data already available on the marketplace.

Benefits of EAN match listing:

* No extensive content creation required
* Minimal technical implementation
* Fast go live for large assortments
* Reduced resource requirements
* Immediate use of marketplace reach

For GenesysAI, this meant a fast entry into marketplace sales without complex IT projects or long integration phases.
